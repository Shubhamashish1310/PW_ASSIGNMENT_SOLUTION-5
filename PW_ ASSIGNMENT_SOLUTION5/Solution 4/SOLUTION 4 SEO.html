Key Statistics

Most ODI Runs

Sachin Tendulkar holds the record for the most ODI runs scored by an Indian player, with 18,426 runs.

Best Bowling Figures

Anil Kumble's 6/12 against the West Indies in 1993 are the best bowling figures by an Indian in ODI cricket.

Memorable Matches

2007 World Cup Victory

India won the 2007 ICC Cricket World Cup under the captaincy of MS Dhoni, defeating Pakistan in the final.

2011 World Cup Triumph

India won the 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup at home, with memorable performances from players like Yuvraj Singh and Gautam Gambhir.

Notable Players

Kapil Dev

Kapil Dev is renowned for leading India to its first World Cup win in 1983. He was a prolific all-rounder.

Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is one of the modern greats, known for his aggressive captaincy and consistent batting performances.
